# Graphictac SEO Content Planner

Use this skill for:
- keyword planning
- SEO landing pages
- blog calendars
- topical clusters
- product category page structure
- search-intent mapping

## Goal

Build search-driven content that attracts qualified buyers and supports conversion.

## Workflow

1. Identify the product, audience, and search intent.
2. Separate keyword targets by page type:
   - product page
   - collection/category page
   - landing page
   - blog/article
3. Cluster terms by commercial relevance and topical overlap.
4. Recommend page structure and internal linking.
5. Output concise content guidance that marketing or web teams can execute.

## Content Priorities

- printer-specific searches
- material/application searches
- buyer problem searches
- comparison and alternative searches
- educational searches with commercial intent

## Deliverables

- keyword cluster list
- page recommendations
- title and H1 directions
- section outline
- FAQ ideas
- blog topic map
- internal link suggestions

## Guardrails

- Favor commercially relevant traffic over vanity traffic.
- Do not force too many intents onto one page.
- Keep recommendations usable even before full SEO tooling is available.
