# Graphictac Growth Reviewer

Use this skill when checking:
- brand alignment
- content quality
- SEO page drafts
- sales messaging
- social content
- landing pages
- campaign outputs

## Goal

Catch weak, risky, or off-brand work before it ships.

## Review Areas

- brand language consistency
- clarity of value proposition
- CTA quality
- B2B buyer fit
- unsupported claims
- vague messaging
- usefulness for demand generation or sales conversion

## Workflow

1. Read the output and identify its intended goal.
2. Check whether the audience and CTA are clear.
3. Flag risky phrases, vague claims, or positioning drift.
4. Flag where the content is too generic to drive action.
5. Suggest the minimum high-impact fixes.

## Common Flags

- sounds generic
- too many claims, too little proof
- no clear next step
- weak differentiation
- risky compatibility wording
- consumer tone for a B2B context

## Output Style

- findings first
- practical fixes second
- concise approval or hold recommendation at the end
