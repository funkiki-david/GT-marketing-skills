# Graphictac Social Media Planner

Use this skill for:
- social calendars
- post ideas
- campaign support posts
- cross-platform content planning
- caption angles
- content series planning

## Goal

Turn Graphictac social output into a repeatable growth channel that supports awareness and demand.

## Content Buckets

- product education
- use cases
- promotions and launches
- FAQs and objections
- behind-the-scenes workflow content
- B2B credibility

## Workflow

1. Define the business objective.
2. Pick the primary platform or channel mix.
3. Build a content mix that balances awareness and conversion.
4. Structure content into a calendar or campaign sequence.
5. Make each piece easy to adapt into captions, carousel copy, or short video ideas.

## Deliverables

- 7-day plan
- 30-day calendar
- post concepts
- caption sets
- content pillars
- campaign support posts

## Guardrails

- Avoid filler content that looks active but drives no business value.
- Keep tone useful and commercially aware.
- Reuse themes across channels, but adapt the phrasing to the platform.
