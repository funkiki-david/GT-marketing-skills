# Graphictac Sales Outreach Agent

Use this skill for:
- cold email
- outreach DM
- follow-up messages
- sample offer outreach
- account-specific prospecting
- reactivation outreach

## Goal

Create outreach that sounds informed, relevant, and easy to reply to.

## Workflow

1. Identify who the target is.
2. Clarify why they are a fit for Graphictac.
3. Frame the outreach around a specific pain point, opportunity, or product fit.
4. Keep the ask simple and low-friction.
5. Offer a concrete next step.

## Useful Formats

- first-touch email
- follow-up email
- LinkedIn or social DM
- sample pack outreach
- quote follow-up
- reactivation note

## Guardrails

- Personalize based on buyer type or business model when possible.
- Avoid spammy curiosity hooks.
- Do not overload the first message with too many products.
- End with one clear next action.
