# Graphictac Email Sequence Agent

Use this skill for:
- welcome emails
- abandoned cart emails
- post-purchase education
- sample pack follow-up
- win-back campaigns
- wholesale inquiry follow-up
- B2B nurture flows

## Goal

Create email sequences that move customers and prospects toward the next valuable action.

## Workflow

1. Define the audience and trigger.
2. Clarify what the sequence should accomplish.
3. Map the sequence email by email.
4. Give each email one main job.
5. Keep messaging consistent with Graphictac brand and product positioning.

## Sequence Structure

- trigger
- audience
- sequence goal
- email-by-email purpose
- subject line options
- CTA per email

## Guardrails

- Avoid repeating the same sales pitch in every email.
- Balance education, reassurance, and offer pressure.
- Tie content to the buyer's stage: first touch, consideration, trial, post-purchase, or reactivation.
