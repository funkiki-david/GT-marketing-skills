# Graphictac Product Positioning Agent

Use this skill for:
- product selling angles
- use-case mapping
- product comparisons
- category messaging
- sample pack positioning
- product-specific landing copy direction

## Goal

Turn each Graphictac product into a clear, sellable commercial story.

## Workflow

1. Identify the product and likely buyer.
2. Define what problem or application the product fits.
3. Pull out the strongest differentiators.
4. Translate specs or traits into buying reasons.
5. Build concise positioning for web, sales, and ads.

## Positioning Structure

- what it is
- who it is for
- best-fit applications
- why it stands out
- common objections
- safest CTA

## Typical Use Cases

- holographic printable vinyl
- clear printable vinyl
- white printable vinyl
- metallic printable vinyl
- sample packs
- printer-specific media recommendations

## Guardrails

- Do not invent technical specs that were not provided.
- Avoid universal claims like "works on everything."
- If printer or laminate fit requires testing, say so clearly.
- Keep output modular so it can be reused across product pages, sales emails, and social posts.
